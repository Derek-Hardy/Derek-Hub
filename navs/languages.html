Languages

I am passionate about learning languages and always have an open mind to embrace cultures of people in other countries.

I believe YouTube is a great place to get exposure to spoken languages and practice listening.

Besides textbook, I always supplement my learning with great videos from local YouTubers that help me improve on the language skills.

Here I would like to recommend a bunch of great YouTubers by language.

You definitely should check them out if you are also interested!

French

Francais avec Pierre
A couple channel that provides extensive learning materials to master French language. They explain many grammatical concepts in detail and show many awesome aspects of French culture. The expressions they use are simple and beginner-friendly.

innerFrench
A French professor living in Poland. He gives very good advice on learning French and produce many great podcasts for listening practice.

French with Tony
A very casual person who is passionate about teaching French in a fun way. He recently left the Easy French channel and start his own (this one). But I feel both channels are awesome to catch up with authentic French words and expressions.

German

Easy German
A professional German learning channel with a couple Janusz & Cari showing you the fun way to learn German on the street. The content is suitable for all levels of German learner.

Goethe-Institut
The official channel of Goethe-Institute. There are many situational conversations that allows German learners to pick up daily expression with authentic German taste.

Learn German with Jenny
A professional German teacher making videos in both English & German for learners of all levels. There are detailed explanations on all grammatical aspects and is a very good starting point for beginners.

English

English Jade - Learn English (engVid)
I personally prefer British accent when started learning English years ago. Jade is a great teacher who focuses on every single details to deliver the most precise contents possible. There are also good videos that reveal British lifestyles & culture.

Learn English with Papa Teach Me
A funny British guy who makes great videos on grammar, pronunciation and accent. The content is easy to understand. Suitable for anyone who's interested in learning British accent or just want to get a taste of British humour.

BBC Learning English
An official BBC learning channel where you can probably get the most authentic British listening resources. Personally I love the News Review programme by Finn & Neil.

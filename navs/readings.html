My readings

I am a voracious book reader since young. Here I want to share some good Computer Science related books that I read during my university.


Ng-Book 2: The Complete Book on Angular 2, by Ari Lerner*

How Google works, by Eric Schmidt & Jonathan Rosenberg
Nice book to get knowing the history of Google as a young and iconic company. The unique work culture and management at Google and its success in the technology field are the key takeaways.

The Four, by Scott Galloway
It shares a good insight of today's technology industry and how Apple, Facebook, Amazon and Google make themselves competitive and dominant.

Architektur-und Entwurfsmuster der Softwaretechnik, by Joachim Goll

Android programming, by Bill Phillips

The first line of code in Android, by Guo lin

Thinking in Java, by Bruce Eckel
